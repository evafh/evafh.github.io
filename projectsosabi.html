Sobat Disabilitas (SOSABI)

SOBAT DISABILITAS is an application that involves four stakeholders (people with disabilities, the general public, the government, and collaborative partners). It is equipped with several features such as Mutual Assistance (Sobat Membantu), Ride-Sharing (Sobat Menebeng), News Updates (Sobat Berkabar), Issue Reporting (Sobat Melapor), Medical Access (Sobat Medis), Job Opportunities (Sobat Kerja), Skills Training (Sobat Melatih), and a Community Hub (Sobat Komunitas). The SOBAT DISABILITAS application was created with the goal of being a solution for the empowerment and the equalization of rights for the disability community.
